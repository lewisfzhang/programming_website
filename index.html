About Us

We are the Programming Club at Bellarmine College Prep. Our goal is to foster interest in computer science. We provide instruction in Java/Python/NodeJS by guiding students through development workshops, where students build their own desktop/web apps. We also train students to participate in competitions and hackathons by teaching data structures and algorithms of varying complexity. Feel free to join us, Thursdays at lunch in Mr. Davis' L-214!
